    About Us Mission

Research

Our research team strives towards an ideal balance of deep product impact and cutting edge academic contributions. We believe in delivering the best value to the customer: via both leveraging the state of the art algorithms (where available) and building our own models ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and productivity. Our research spans across a range of 5 areas:

Natural Language Processing & Information Retrieval

Unstructured data processing involves dealing with a large amount of text in several forms. We build state of the art models for natural language understanding, information extraction, natural language generation, and language representation learning using the latest LLMs and Gen-AI technologies.

Multimodal Content Understanding

Information regarding enterprise content is represented in the form of documents and other multimodal (images, markdowns, datasheets) formats. In addition to the text, layout and structural arrangement augments the semantics of enterprise content. Our intelligence engine based on proprietary algorithms for document image understanding, document processing, multimodal modal language models focuses on groundbreaking inventions for increased productivity and accuracy for RPA processes.

Computer Vision

Visual information processing is critical towards dealing with real world enterprise data. Our team invests in leveraging the latest image segmentation and image tagging models that integrate with the NLP pipeline to bring in the rich information across different modalities.

Machine learning for end to end Automation

Ensuring that the right data is identified, cleaning the incoming data and documents, routing them to the right approval, and finally flagging for any alerts in the process are critical steps in the RPA pipeline. We are building ML tools and algorithms that introduce true end-end automation in turn accelerating the speed and efficiency with minimal human intervention in day to day enterprise processes.

COGs research for workflow optimisation

We have dedicated focus on ensuring minimal cost and high speed processing via investment in AI-based optimizations across the RPA workflow.
