About the Company

HYPRBOTS

Hyprbots Systems is developing a series of autonomous enterprise robots. The company is building an unstructured data processing platform (UDP). This UDP is used as a foundation to create hyper-intelligent software robots for various horizontal business processes of an enterprise. The goal is to use NLP, ML, and computer vision to make business process-specific contextual output. UDP APIs get integrated into enterprise systems for HRM, ERP, CRM, Accounting, IT operations, and DevOps functions. The integration layer has business process-specific nuances abstracted to achieve the desired outcomes. All of this is stitched together by the robotic process automation orchestrator.

The hyper-intelligent bots mimic all human tasks end-to-end for a given business process. These hyper bots give optimal control to humans with information and approval loops.

The company's mission is to be a world leader in hyper-automation for small and medium enterprises by capturing 30% of market share through these end-to-end ready-to-deploy bots. The company foresees the deployment of a million bots freeing a few million humans from mundane tasks in finance, HR, DevOps, and admin functions over the next 5 years. In the process, it will build original intellectual property with dozens of patents and publications changing the way small and medium enterprises need to operate in the 21st century with AI workers. We also envision being a 100 MUSD revenue run-rate company at the end of 5th year.
