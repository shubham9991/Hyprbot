Account Payable
Hyper Assistant

AP Hyper Assistant makes account payable finance function fully autonomus. However it continues to remain in full control of human accountants. The tasks of invoice identification, classification, attachment extraction, reading and validating invoice information, information extraction, extracted information entry into general ledger (ERP system ) are transferred to this assitant with human accounts in the loop on need basis to review and approve.

Account Receivable
Hyper Assistant

AR Hyper Assistant makes account recievable finance function fully autonomus. However it continues to remain in full control of human accountants. The tasks of contract reading, invoice generation and validation, email despatch, customer follow-ups, payment acknowledgement, and read/write of invoice/payment information into general ledger (ERP and Payment Systems) are transferred to this assistant. Human accountants and sales representatives are in loop on need basis to review and approve.

Claims Processing Hyper Assistant

CP Hyper Assistant makes employee claim processing function fully autonomus. However it continues to remain in full control of humans. The tasks of claim policy reading, rules formation, claims amount validation agaunst policy, information extractions from bills of proofs, validation of claimed amounts against proofs, write of claim information into general ledger (ERP and Payment Systems) and payment initiations are transferred to this assistant. Human accountants and line managers are in loop to review and approve.

Recruitment Processing Hyper Assistant

RP Hyper Assistant makes recruitment function autonomus. However it is controlled by humans in HR function. The tasks of creating and refining job description, searching and matching profiles, setting up interviews, communicating line managers and candidates and automating offer creation are transferre dto this assistant. Human resource staff and line managers are in loop to review and approve.

Contract and Legal Hyper Assistant

CAL Hyper Assistant makes contract management function semi-autonomous. The tasks of creating and refining contracts, risk assessment, keeping track of renewals, keeping trcak of parity, updating the contract, collaborating with other hyper assistants from finance and HT functions, are transferred to this assistant. The legal staff and managers in contract function are in loop to review and approve.

General Purpose Office Hyper Assistant

GPO Hyper Assistant makes general office function semi-autonomous. The tasks of creating and drafting letters, reports, prepearing presentations, arranging meeting are assisted by this assistant. The office clerks review and approve the output.

Cloud Capacity Monitoring and Control Hyper Assistant

CCMC Hyper Assistant makes cloud monitoring and cost control function autonomous. The tasks of understanding events and logs of applications, predicting and planning VM and storage loads, taking human approvals whereever needed, and managing GCC, AWS and Azures resources using respective management tools would be transferred to this assistant. Production and Devops human resources will control the assistant's actions.
