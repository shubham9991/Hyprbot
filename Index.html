Challenges

with Existing Automation Solutions
01

Partial Automation

No one is giving me “ready to deploy” solution that automates my process end to end. Like in account payable, no one offers solution to automate my tasks involved in all three areas i.e. front office systems(supplier), document processing and back office systems(finance)

02

Very High System Integration Effort

The current vendors give a platform that requires system integration effort ranging from 3 months to 12 months with several technical resources. The budget for such large effort is a big constraint.

03

Higher Operating Costs

The current vendors’ technology is computing heavy resulting into higher licensing and infrastructure costs. Moderate automation accuracy results into low productivity in the business process. Overall ROI is moderate to low.

04

Difficulty in Change Management

The current solutions offer workflow tools that bind tasks in rigid sequence with fixed inputs and outputs. Business requires changes in them from time to time. These changes need technical resources with platform knowledge. This is expensive, and leads to no-change situation on ground.
